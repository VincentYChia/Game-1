## SKILL EFFECT TYPES

All skills use one of these 10 effect types:

1. **empower** - Increases effectiveness/power (damage, quality, output)
2. **quicken** - Increases speed/reduces time (attack speed, crafting time, movement)
3. **fortify** - Increases resistance/durability (defense, tool preservation)
4. **restore** - Recovers/heals (HP, durability, mana)
5. **pierce** - Critical hits/armor penetration (crit chance, accuracy)
6. **elevate** - Increases rarity/quality tier (rare drops, quality upgrades)
7. **enrich** - Increases yield/quantity (bonus materials, extra drops)
8. **regenerate** - Over-time recovery (HP regen, durability regen)
9. **devastate** - Area-of-effect damage/effects (AOE attacks, chain harvest)
10. **transcend** - Bypass restrictions/limitations (tier bypass, requirement bypass)
